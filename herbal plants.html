Explore the Benefits of Herbal and AYUSH Plants

Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Uses: Known for boosting immunity, reducing stress, and aiding digestion.

Merits: Natural antioxidant, helps fight respiratory infections and provides anti-inflammatory effects.

Ashwagandha

Uses: Commonly used to reduce stress, improve sleep, and boost overall vitality.

Merits: Known as an adaptogen that helps the body resist physical and mental stress.

Neem

Uses: Used for skin health, immune support, and oral hygiene.

Merits: Antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties make it a versatile herb.

Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Uses: Enhances immunity, promotes digestion, and supports heart health.

Merits: Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants, supports eye health and rejuvenates cells.

Aloe Vera

Uses: Commonly used for skin care, digestive health, and wound healing.

Merits: Soothes burns, hydrates skin, and promotes gut health.

Brahmi

Uses: Enhances memory, reduces anxiety, and improves cognitive functions.

Merits: Supports brain health and has neuroprotective properties.

Turmeric

Uses: Known for anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits.

Merits: Supports joint health, improves skin, and boosts immunity.

Ginger

Uses: Used for digestive health, nausea relief, and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Merits: Eases digestive discomfort, reduces inflammation, and enhances immunity.
