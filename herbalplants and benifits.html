Herbal Plants and Their Benefits

Discover the natural healing power of herbs!

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is known for its soothing properties, great for skin care, and promotes healing of cuts and burns.

Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Tulsi helps boost immunity, reduces stress, and supports respiratory health.

Mint

Mint is refreshing, aids digestion, and helps relieve headaches.

Ginger

Ginger is effective against nausea, fights infections, and reduces inflammation.
