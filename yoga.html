Welcome to the Virtual Herbal Garden. Explore different yoga exercises and learn their benefits.

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

How to Do: Stand tall with feet together, hands at sides. Engage your core, lift through your thighs, and hold.

Benefits: Improves posture, balance, and promotes mental clarity.

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

How to Do: Start on all fours, lift your hips, and push back into an inverted “V” position.

Benefits: Strengthens arms and legs, stretches the spine, and relieves tension.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)

How to Do: Step one foot back, bend the front knee, and raise arms overhead.

Benefits: Increases stamina, balance, and energizes the body.

Child's Pose (Balasana)

How to Do: Kneel down, sit back on your heels, and stretch your arms forward on the floor.

Benefits: Calms the mind, relieves back pain, and stretches the hips.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

How to Do: Stand on one leg, place the other foot on your inner thigh, and raise your arms overhead.

Benefits: Enhances balance and strengthens the legs and core.

Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

How to Do: Lie on your back, bend your knees, and lift your hips while keeping feet flat on the floor.

Benefits: Opens the chest, strengthens back muscles, and reduces anxiety.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

How to Do: Lie face down, place hands under shoulders, and lift the chest off the ground.

Benefits: Stretches the spine, strengthens the back, and improves flexibility.

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

How to Do: Stand with legs apart, reach one hand to the ground while extending the other arm upward.

Benefits: Increases flexibility, strengthens legs, and relieves back pain.

Boat Pose (Navasana)

How to Do: Sit with knees bent, lift feet, and balance on your sit bones, extending arms forward.

Benefits: Strengthens the core, improves balance, and promotes concentration.

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

How to Do: Lie flat on your back, relax your body, and focus on breathing.

Benefits: Reduces stress, relaxes muscles, and promotes overall relaxation.
